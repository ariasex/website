AloisNL Toplane fundamentals course

My Goal

In general, my coaching has been successful with 90% of my students across all ranks and champions. My goal is to assist everyone in improving their toplane skills in the short and long term by focusing on fundamentals.

What is it?

This course is conducted in a group setting on Discord where topics are covered through screen sharing. By putting in effort and applying the taught concepts, I am confident that students will see improvement in their gameplay and climb the ranks. These concepts can be applied to any champion. All sessions will be recorded, allowing for review and questions in between sessions. Questions can also be asked during the sessions, with a structured approach to ensure everyone has a chance to ask.

When is it?

Now

Session structure

Session 1

  • Climbing in toplane, mindset & goals
  • How to play aggressive
  • Mastering the early laning phase and the importance of the first 4 waves

Session 2

  • Repeat session 1 topics
  • How to get and expand a lead
  • How to impact the map

Sessions 3

  • How to appraoch mid to late game
  • How to consistently get high farm average
  • Sidewaves, jungle camps or grouping?
  • How to approach neutral objectives

Session 4

  • comprehensive review of all covered material and a detailed analysis of full games.
