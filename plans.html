Which plan is right for me?

Vod Review

If you are new to my coaching, this vod review session is an excellent starting point. To get started, please provide 2-3 vods from your point of view, recorded and uploaded to YouTube with a minimum quality of 720P. You can use outplayed.gg to record and upload your games to YouTube effortlessly. During our first session, I will focus on the early laning phase and establish a solid foundation in the game's fundamentals. It's best to select vods where you either break even or come out ahead to analyze and enhance your laning phase. Feel free to reach out to me on Discord at AloisNL#2650 if you have any questions.

Live Game

This session is perfect if we have already worked together or if you have a good grasp of the fundamentals. It is a live coaching session where we begin with a brief introduction, followed by a review of your op.gg profile to discuss your rune and build choices, as well as your goals for playing League. You can ask questions, and I may ask some in return. After that, you will join a live game, and I will spectate your Discord stream, guiding you on decisions such as item builds, base timings, and lane assignments throughout the game. Later, we will analyze the game and your decisions, focusing on general goals in League. This approach allows us to identify negative habits and establish new goals for improvement. If you have any additional questions, feel free to add me on Discord: AloisNL#2650.

Training plans

The training plans are personalized and tailored to your individual needs, incorporating a mix of the vod review and live game review sessions mentioned above. The key difference is that I will also serve as your mentor, guiding and supporting you every step of the way to reach your desired goals. You can reach out to me at any time with questions or concerns between sessions as we work together to improve your gameplay.
